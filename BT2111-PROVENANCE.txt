BT.2111 HDR clips
=================

The authoritative in-repository generator is the Rust `bt2111-gen` tool in
`src/`. It writes raw Y4M `yuv420p10le` frames directly and has no Rust crate
dependencies. Use `generate-bt2111.sh` to generate PQ and HLG Y4M files and
then encode them with ffmpeg.

The existing VP9/WebM files are:

  bt2111-pq-1920x1080-libvpx-vp9-yuv420p10.webm
  bt2111-hlg-1920x1080-libvpx-vp9-yuv420p10.webm

Historical note: the first copies of these files were generated from
test-full-band/tfb-video as an external build-time reference tool. The GPL-3
generator source was not vendored here. The Rust generator replaces that
temporary provenance.
